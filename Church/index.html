The Methodist Church of Ghana

Northern Accra Diocese

The Northern Accra Diocese (NAD) is the twentieth Diocese of the Methodist Church Ghana (MCG). It was officially created on 1stOctober, 2018.The Madina Circuit is the head circuit of the Diocese and the Ebenezer Cathedral at Madina is the Diocesan Cathedral. The following fifteen (15) circuits constituting the Northern Accra Diocese at its inception are:


  • Adenta Circuit
  • Airport East Circuit
  • Akweteman Circuit
  • Ashaley Botwe Circuit
  • Awoshie Circuit
  • Dzorwulu Circuit
  • Ga-Sub Urban Circuit
  • Gbawe McCarthy Hill Circuit
  • Kwabenya Circuit
  • Kwashieman North Circuit
  • Madina Circuit
  • New Achimota Circuit
  • Ngleshie-Amanfrom Circuit
  • Nii Boiman Circuit
  • Taifa Circuit
There are various organizations in the church,Some are;the choir,singing band,christ litlle band and many others.
